Name of the business: Lee Family Farms

Project: Lee Family Farms, a local family-owned salad farm, approached us to create a logo that reflects the fresh, wholesome nature of their produce. Our design features a charming, doodled salad paired with elegant cursive letters, symbolizing both the simplicity and quality of their farm-to-table offerings. The logo embodies the organic and fresh values of the farm and highlights their dedication to sustainability and community. We aimed to create a visual identity that stands out in the market while representing the farm's commitment to these principles.

Name of the business: Maple Ridge Farms

Project: Maple Ridge Farms, known for their diverse range of vegetables and ethically raised animals, sought a logo that harmonizes the rich elements of their farm life. Our design features a red barn standing proudly on a verdant, tilled farm, with majestic fir trees providing a scenic backdrop. This logo represents the farm's dedication to sustainable agriculture and animal welfare. We aimed to craft a visual identity that not only captures the picturesque charm of Maple Ridge Farms but also highlights their commitment to quality and care.

Name of the business: PetPals Grooming

Project: PetPals Grooming, a premier pet grooming service, came to us with the vision of creating a logo that seamlessly blends professionalism with a friendly touch. At the heart of the logo is an adorable hand-drawn poodle, symbolizing the care and attention given to each pet. This visual identity aims to stand out in the pet grooming industry while highlighting PetPals Grooming's dedication to top-notch care and a welcoming atmosphere
